# Nano]°[Biostructures Research Group

## About Us
The **Nano]°[Biostructures RG** is part of the Unit of Experimental Medicine at the Institute for Obesity Research (IOR) at Tecnológico de Monterrey. Our research focuses on identifying novel molecular scaffolds and characterizing them as potential ligands for receptors, enzymes, ion channels, and transporters involved in metabolic disorders and related diseases.

This document is aimed to show the research group website and project portfolio.

Visit our website at [NanoBiostructuresRG](https://sites.google.com/tec.mx/nanobiostructures)
